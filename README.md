# AI Governance Dashboard

## Project Overview

The AI Governance Dashboard is designed to monitor, manage, and evaluate AI systems through a structured governance framework focused on risk, compliance, transparency, accountability, and responsible AI practices.

This project demonstrates how organizations can implement governance controls to ensure safe, ethical, and compliant deployment of AI solutions.

---

## Business Problem

As organizations adopt Generative AI and Agentic AI solutions, they face challenges related to:

- AI hallucinations
- Model risks
- Regulatory compliance
- Data privacy concerns
- Bias and fairness issues
- Auditability requirements

Without proper governance mechanisms, AI systems may introduce operational, compliance, financial, and reputational risks.

---

## Project Objectives

- Monitor AI risks and governance metrics
- Track AI compliance requirements
- Measure model performance
- Improve transparency and accountability
- Support responsible AI adoption
- Provide executive-level governance reporting
